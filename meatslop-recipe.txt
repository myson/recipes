Ingredients:
 - 2 lb ground meat
 - 1 onion
 - 1 head cabbage
 - 1 head garlic
 - 2 cans tomato paste
 - olive oil or other cooking fat
 - mushrooms, broccoli, etc (optional)

 Directions:
 	1. Chop up cabbage, onion, garlic.

 	2. Heat oil in large pot.

 	3. Add vegetables.

 	4. Add meat.

 	5. Add tomato paste and stir.

 	6. Stir carefully until meat is fully cooked.
